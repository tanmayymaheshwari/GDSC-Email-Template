What is GDSC?

Google Developer Student Clubs (GDSC) are community groups specifically designed for college and university students who have an interest in Google developer technologies.

We welcome students from all undergraduate or graduate programs who are looking to enhance their skills as developers. By becoming a member of GDSC IIITM, students have the opportunity to expand their knowledge in a collaborative learning environment and create solutions for local businesses and their community.

Learn more about it here.

Joining the Community

As a core team member

1. If you are enthusiastic about uplifting the developer spirit within our institute.
2. If you wish to share your knowledge and skills with your peers
3. If you're interested in organising amazing events to up skill and empower developers in our institute and help them showcase their skills at a global level
4. If you're willing to spare a considerable amount of time apart from your academic and personal schedule for the club and in-turn the community.

As a community member

1. If you want to be a part of a vibrant community of developers spread across the globe.
2. If you want to learn about amazing technologies from fellow peers and Google Experts and grow as a developer.
3. If you want to share your knowledge and help your peers become better developers.
4. If you're interested in building impactful products for the community.

While there are limited positions available for core team members, anyone can join GDSC - IIITM as a community member at any time. It's important to note that technical skills are not the primary criteria for selection as a core team member. The main factors considered are your willingness and determination to learn and contribute to the community, alongside the community.

If you're interested in joining GDSC - IIITM as a core team member, please complete the form provided.
Remember, technical skills are not the main focus for core team selection; it's your passion and commitment to learning and contributing to the community that matters most.
